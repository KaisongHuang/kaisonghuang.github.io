About Me

I am a 4th-Year PhD Student advised by Dr. Tianzheng Wang at Simon Fraser University / Data-Intensive Systems Lab. My research interests are mainly focused on database engines, transaction processing, and storage management. Previously, I obtained MMath in Computer Science and BEng in Software Engineering from Univesity of Waterloo and Nanjing University, respectively.
