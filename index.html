About Me (I'm open to a full-time position in 2025.)

I am a PhD candidate advised by Prof. Tianzheng Wang at Simon Fraser University / Data-Intensive Systems Lab. My research interests are mainly focused on database engines, transaction processing, storage management, and disaggregated systems. Previously, I obtained MMath in Computer Science and BEng in Software Engineering from University of Waterloo and Nanjing University, respectively. I received ACM SIGMOD Research Highlight Award in 2023.
