About Me

I am a 3rd-Year PhD Student advised by Dr. Tianzheng Wang in the Data-Intensive Systems Lab at Simon Fraser University. My research interests are mainly focused on database engines, transaction processing, and storage management. You can find me in my lab TASC1 9400 or via email: kha85@sfu.ca.
